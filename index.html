About Me.

Hi! I'm a master's student at the UCSF Health Data Science Program. I build, calibrate and find trends from brain signals, protein structure, and clinical trials using R, Python, and C++. I use statistical analyses and machine learning methods to find correlations from clinical designs. My current interest focuses on precision health from large language models and natural language processing following topic analyses and VADER Sentiment for finding unknown symptoms from drugs and healthcare products. I hope to advance my work into academia to maximize my potential at the biggest stage. I also involve in Japanese illustration and web graphic design as a hobby posting artworks on PIXIV.
