About Me.

Hi! I'm a current master's student at UCSF Health Data Science. I conduct, build, calibrate, and standardize health datasets from brain signals, protein structure, disease transmission populations, and clinical trials using R, python, MATLAB, and C++. I formulate mathematical models in predicting disease transmission and MRI imaging. I am currently researching on machine incorporation in precision healthcare from Large Language Models and Natural Language Processing. I hope following my work into academia to maximize my potential at the biggest stage. I also make UI/UX interfaces with Japanese Illustration and graphic Design.
